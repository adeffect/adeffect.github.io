Measuring Heterogeneity in TV Advertising Elasticities: Evidence from 135 Retail and Restaurant Brands

Samsun Knight, Tsung-Yiou Hsieh, and Yakov Bart — Forthcoming in the Journal of Marketing Research

Overview

This dashboard presents an interactive summary of the heterogeneity in advertising response documented in the paper. The goal is to make transparent how advertising effectiveness varies across markets and how observable market characteristics moderate these responses, beyond what is captured by average treatment effects.

⚠️ All results shown are based on point estimates. Uncertainty measures (e.g., confidence intervals) will be incorporated in a future update.

Interpretation Notes

The dashboard visualizes estimated heterogeneity. All coefficients and elasticities are based on the estimated advertising response model. The purpose of this dashboard is to provide intuition for the economic relevance of heterogeneity, rather than to perform estimation or optimization.
